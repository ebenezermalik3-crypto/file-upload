Our Story

1999

The Beginning

Founded by master mechanic Robert AutoCraft with a simple vision: combine old-school craftsmanship with modern technology to deliver transparent, reliable automotive service.

2010

Expansion & Innovation

Moved to our current facility and invested heavily in diagnostic equipment and team training. Became one of the first shops to offer real-time repair updates to customers.

2025

Industry Leadership

Today, we're recognized as the region's premier automotive service center, with advanced hybrid and electric vehicle capabilities and a customer satisfaction rate of 98%.
