Common Issues Guide

Understanding common automotive problems and when professional service is necessary.

Engine Problems

Strange noises, rough idling, loss of power, or check engine lights.

⚠️ Requires immediate professional attention

Brake Issues

Squealing, grinding, soft pedal, or pulling to one side.

🚨 Safety critical - immediate service required

Transmission Problems

Slipping, hard shifting, or unusual transmission noises.

⚠️ Schedule service soon to prevent major damage

Routine Maintenance

Oil changes, filter replacements, and scheduled services.

✓ Preventive care - schedule at your convenience
